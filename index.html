Brooke Oliver

I grew up a Navy brat. Traveling from the east coast to the west coast. Due to the constant moving and not being to always have keep in contact with friends via the phone. I started using the household computer as a way to communicate with friends who I had to leave. What once was a way to communicate to friends soon turned into me exploring the internet. Finding information on things I needed, learning new techniques, reading global news. My love for computers started from there.
